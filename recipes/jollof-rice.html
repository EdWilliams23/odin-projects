Jollof Recipe

Spicy Jollof

This recipe will teach you how to make a tasty jollof rice which will impress your friends and family using the freshest ingredients.

Ingredients List

  • Chicken Breast
  • Rice
  • Curry powder
  • Scotch bonnet pepper
  • Oninon

    • Steps:

    1. Boil rice and add butter
    2. Cook chicken with curry powder
    3. add cream to chicken
