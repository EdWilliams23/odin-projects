Delicious Vindaloo

This recipe will teach you how to make a banging vindaloo which will impress your friends and family using the freshest ingredients.

Ingredients List

  • Rice
  • Lamb chops
  • Tomato sauce

    • Steps:

    1. Boild rice and place in bowl
    2. Cook lamb with tomato sauce
    3. add cream to lamb and combine with rice
